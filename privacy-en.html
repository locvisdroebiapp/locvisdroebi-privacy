Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 Sep 2025

This policy explains how “Locvisdroebi (Prayer Times)” processes data. The app does not create user accounts and does not sell or share personal data.

Data We Process

  • Location: Used to compute prayer times. Processing is on-device. If auto‑location is enabled, coordinates may be sent over HTTPS to our proxy only to resolve the nearest city ID and fetch weekly times. Coordinates are not persistently stored.
  • On‑device cache: Preferences and the latest prayer times are stored locally for offline display.
  • Server technical logs: For reliability and rate limiting, our server may temporarily process technical data (e.g., IP address, timestamp, request path, status code) and keep it for a short period.

Purpose

  • Provide accurate prayer times for your selected/current city.
  • Schedule notifications correctly.
  • Maintain reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Prayer times are fetched from the Turkish Diyanet public API via our proxy; requests include only the minimum data (city ID or temporary coordinates).

Storage & Security

  • No persistent personal identifiers are stored.
  • Technical logs are retained briefly and then deleted.
  • All transport uses HTTPS.

Your Choices

  • You may disable location and select a city manually.
  • You may clear the app cache from system settings.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13.
